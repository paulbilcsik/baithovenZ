China - Wenzhou

Hello everyone, my name is Paul and I'm 21 years old. I have been to China recently. This was one of the most spiritual and purest experiences I have ever had.

City

Wenzhou-City is loud and colorful. It's confusing and impressive at the same time. It is a very special place where you feel immediatly attracted. Once you have been here you do not want to leave.

Countryside

Wenzhou is located in a mountainous region on the Ou River. Nature is incredibly diverse and monumentally extraordinary. There are old harbor and mountain villages that are still completely untouched by any urban influence.

Culture

Wenzhou people are very proud of their history and culture. They played a big role in the liberation of China during the Japanese siege. Throughout China, Wenzhou is known for the strict dialect spoken here.
